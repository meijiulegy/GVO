Calibration Method 3

Instructions:

Fixate the chosen test eye on the yellow target and cover the other eye

Upon clicking the 'Start Calibration' button, a red dot will appear and start moving away from the yellow target.

With test eye fixated on the yellow target, press spacebar when the red dot disappears.

Repeat this process 3 times by pressing "Repeat" while keeping your head still.

You may redo this step if you wish by pressing the "Redo Calibration" button.
